# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Gesture Keyboard ("the App") respects your privacy and is designed to protect your data.

## Data Collection

The App does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or typing information.

All text input (including keystrokes and gestures) is processed entirely on your device.

## On-Device Processing

The App uses a prebuilt language model (e.g., a bigram model) to provide word predictions.  
This model runs entirely on-device and does NOT learn from, store, or transmit your typing data.

## Keyboard Permissions (Full Access)

The keyboard may request “Allow Full Access” in iOS settings to enable certain features, such as voice feedback.

Even when Full Access is enabled:
- The App does NOT record or transmit what you type
- No keystroke data is sent to any server
- All processing remains local to your device

## Audio / Voice Feedback

The App may use system audio services (e.g., AVFoundation) to provide voice feedback.  
No audio recordings are stored or transmitted.

## Third-Party Services

The App does NOT use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Sharing

The App does NOT share any user data with third parties.

## Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

XXX Keyboard ("the App") respects your privacy and is designed to protect your data.

## Data Collection

The App does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share personal data, typing data, Braille input, gesture input, or keystroke information.

All text input is processed entirely on your device.

## On-Device Processing

The App uses on-device models and decoding logic, such as word prediction and Braille auto-correction, to support text entry.

These features run entirely on-device and do NOT learn from, store, or transmit your typing data.

## Keyboard Permissions (Full Access)

The keyboard may request “Allow Full Access” in iOS settings to enable certain features, such as voice feedback.

Even when Full Access is enabled:
- The App does NOT record or transmit what you type
- No keystroke, gesture, or Braille input data is sent to any server
- All typing-related processing remains local to your device

## Audio / Voice Feedback

The App may use system audio services (such as AVFoundation) to provide voice feedback.

The App does NOT record, store, or transmit audio.

## Third-Party Services

The App does NOT use third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or external typing services.

## Data Sharing

The App does NOT share user data with third parties.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be posted on this page.
